The Challenge

Our Team Was Tasked With Translating Complex Product Requirements Into Development-Ready Deliverables Within Just One Month, With Minimal Official Visual Assets Available.

✨

Highlights

01/

An Animated Entry That Draws Users In

Inspired By The Met Gala Dress Code The Garden of Time, We Incorporated A Rotating Kaleidoscope Visual Motif. The Experience Opens With An Entrance Animation, Uses Motion To Present Rewards, And Builds Curiosity.

02/

Vote, Compete, Unlock

Users Could First Vote On Past Met Gala Looks, Then Vote On Current Looks Once The Event Launches.
A Competitive Card Interaction Adds Playfulness And Implies That Selecting One Option Discards The Other.

03/

Designing For Continued Participation

After The Initial 5 Votes Are Used, Users Must Complete Tasks To Earn More Voting Chances. The Ranking Of Their Favorite Look Is Revealed Only After All Votes Are Cast.

04/

Rewards & Compete With Your Friends

See What Rewards You've Earned, Compete With Friends On The Leaderboard, And Discover Others Who Share Your Taste—Encouraging Sharing And Connection.
