Whirlpool Zero · Statement Machine

Designed to

A sculptural statement washer that transforms everyday laundry into a subtle ritual, combining functional artistry, expressive ambient lighting, and a presence designed to be seen.

Scroll
Functional Artistry

Where function becomes form.

The dual door openings transform the start of each cycle into a subtle spectacle — adding intention, delight, and just the right amount of drama to your daily routine.

Ambient Lighting

Light as the interface.

Expressive ambient lighting runs along the sides of the machine — shifting with cycle state and responding to touch. Which adds personality to your space, and also indicates the stage of the laundry cycle through customizable colors.

Sculptural Form

A presence you choose to notice.

The Statement Machine adopts a silhouette that plays between tension and grace — drawing the eye, then receding into the daily rhythm of the home. It is an object with a point of view.
