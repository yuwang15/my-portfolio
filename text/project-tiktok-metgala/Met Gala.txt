Context As the lead sponsor of the 2024 Met Gala and The Costume Institute’s exhibition Sleeping Beauties: Reawakening Fashion, TikTok sought to extend the event experience beyond livestream viewing and into interactive platform engagement.🤩 The Challenge Our team was tasked with translating complex product requirements into development-ready deliverables within just one month, with minimal official visual assets available. ⏳ tight Timeline 🧩 Multi-Phase Interaction Logic ✨ High Visual Bar with Limited Assets Had only one month to convert complex requirements into finalized, developer-ready designs. Designed a multi-phase experience integrating voting, rewards, rankings, and timed content transitions. Established a fashion-level aesthetic with only a few official brand visual assets. Highlights 01/ An Animated Entry That Draws Users In Inspired by the Met Gala dress code The Garden of Time, we incorporated a rotating kaleidoscope visual motif. The experience opens with an entrance animation, uses motion to present rewards, and builds curiosity. 02/ Vote, Compete, Unlock Users could first vote on past Met Gala looks , then vote on current looks once the event launches.  A competitive card interaction adds playfulness and implies that selecting one option discards the other.  03/ Designing for Continued Participation After the initial 5 votes are used, users must complete tasks to earn more voting chances. The ranking of their favorite look is revealed only after all votes are cast. 04/ rewards & compete with your friends See what rewards you’ve earned, compete with friends on the leaderboard, and discover others who share your taste—encouraging sharing and connection. share my favorite look

My biggest learnings Collaborating in a fast-paced environment with designs that impact millions have taught me… Iterate, fail, learn, repeat! Design Under Pressure There’s always room to improve. I’ve learned to move fast - iterate, learn, and repeat - while seeking experienced perspectives and asking for feedback strategically. Working in high-expectation, high-pressure environments strengthened my resilience and discipline. The more intense the situation, the more essential it becomes to clarify the logic, move step by step according to plan. Transparent Communication & Team Alignment Not all requirements are equal. Proactive and transparent communication keeps teams aligned and focused. Cross-functional collaboration often brings unexpected support and valuable insights that refine and elevate the final design. In complex projects, the ability to prioritize what matters most determines the strength of the final outcome.
